Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the charges.

What happens if I exceed my email limit?

We'll notify you when you reach 80% of your limit. You can either upgrade your plan or purchase additional email credits at $0.50 per 1,000 emails.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. Enterprise customers can pay via invoice.
